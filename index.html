About Us

At CipherWorks, we believe that history's greatest stories make for the most compelling games. We specialize in creating immersive social deduction experiences that transport players to pivotal moments in history, where every decision could change the course of events.

Our mission is to blend engaging gameplay with educational value, allowing players to experience historical events through the lens of those who lived them. Each episode of CIPHER: Shadows of History is meticulously researched to deliver an authentic yet entertaining experience.
